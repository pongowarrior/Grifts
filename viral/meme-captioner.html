How does it work?
This tool uses the browser's native **Canvas API** to draw your image, then draws the text on top using the classic IMPACT font look (white text, black stroke). All processing happens instantly on your device.

What formats are supported?
You can upload JPG, PNG, or WEBP files. The output is a high-quality **PNG** file that is ready to post on X, Instagram, Reddit, or Discord.
