üìù Basic Formula

DPS = (Base Damage √ó Attacks/Second) √ó (1 + Bonus%) √ó Crit Factor √ó (1 - Armor%)

üéØ What Each Stat Means

  • Base Damage: The raw damage value shown on your weapon or ability
  • Attack Speed: How many times you attack per second (e.g., 1.5 APS = attack every 0.67 seconds)
  • Crit Chance: Probability (0-100%) that an attack will be a critical hit
  • Crit Multiplier: How much extra damage crits deal (2.0 = double damage, 2.5 = 2.5x damage)
  • Damage Bonus: Additional % increase from all sources (buffs, passives, gear)
  • Armor/Resistance: How much damage the enemy reduces

üí™ Pro Tips

  • Balance crit chance and crit multiplier for optimal DPS gains
  • Attack speed scales linearly with DPS - doubling APS doubles damage
  • High enemy armor makes % armor penetration extremely valuable
  • Save multiple builds to compare gear upgrades easily
